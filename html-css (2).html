NAVI

NATUS VINCERE commonly referred as abbreviated name NAVI (formerly Na`Vi), is an esports organization. Founded in 2009, the organization has teams and players competing in various games, such as Counter-Strike 2, Dota 2, FIFA, Brawl Stars, World of Tanks, Paladins, PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, Clash of Clans, Apex Legends, Rainbow Six Siege, Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, Fortnite, and VALORANT.

 

Хронология становления NAVI

15–17 January 2010 - IEM 4 European Finals

30–31 January 2010 - Arbalet Asia

3 April 2010 - EM 4 World Championship Finals (W3C)

28–29 August 2010 - ASUS Summer 2010

25–27 November 2010 - Dreamhack Winter 2010

17–21 August 2011 - The International 2011

8 July 2014 – 21 July 2014 - The International 2014

21 November 2015 – 22 November 2015 - IEM Season X - San Jose
